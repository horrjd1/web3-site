INSPIRATIONS

Some sites that I used for inspiration for my site design

HUEMOR

The first site I found for inspiration was huemor.rocks. What really stood out to me was the header, and how bold it was. I dont have any artwork as impressive as that so I decided to go for a gradient instead, as its a little less in your face. The part that really sells the header for me though is the curve at the bottom and how the sections under the header (body sections) are also split using curves. It just stands out a bit more from other sites that use rectangles for everything.

Firefox

What really strikes me about the download page for Firefox is the colour palette. The dark purple and the orange - red - pink/purple gradients. Its sticking to its set colours which makes the page look consistent, but the gradient also gives the impression of more variety/colourful/new and shiny ________
